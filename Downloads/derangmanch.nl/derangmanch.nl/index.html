About Us

De Rangmanch is an initiative to celebrate occasions. It started with the vision to launch a cross - cultural event organization platform in the Netherlands.Creativity belives in opportunity a chance to promote our Indian Culture in Netherland.

Culture is a strong part of people lives. It influences their views, their values, their humor, their hopes, their loyalties, their worries and fears, so when you are working with people and building relationships with them, it helps to have some perspective and understanding of their cultures. We facilitate a variety of events for families and kids.To make you feel home away from home.

It will provide opportunity specially to the children to learn about their culture.This is amazing platform to entertain, educate and learn way to happy life. De Rangmanch is the best platform where we relate our children to our culture, our nation and they enjoy alot.
Our goal is to make every event successful through our commitment to quality service & top of the line equipments. Our commitment to producing events of the highest standard is at the forefront of everything we do.

The Ramayana

We Will Be Bringning Lots Of Excitement Your Way!

The Ramayana is one of the largest ancient epics in world literature which celebrates the victory of good over evil, which epitomizes love, courage, devotion, karma and sacrifice in the purest form. After the huge success in our first show, De Rangmanch brings the epic saga of Ramayana once again in Netherlands in the form of musical drama comprising of accomplished talented artists from across India.
